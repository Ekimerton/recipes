# Teriyaki Salad Dressing

A sweet, tangy, and garlicky Japanese-style dressing made famous by Pacific Northwest teriyaki shops. Featuring a rich Kewpie mayonnaise base whipped to a light consistency, this versatile sauce is perfect for drizzling over fresh salad greens, grilled chicken, and steamed rice.

- **Source:** [Original Recipe](https://youtu.be/CE2ceMKvXtk?is=u7KM-LAX48H09OFA&t=230s)

## Ingredients

- 1/2 cup Kewpie mayonnaise
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 3 tbsp sugar
- 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 splash sesame oil
- 1 tbsp minced garlic

## Instructions

1. Add the Kewpie mayonnaise, rice vinegar, sugar, soy sauce, sesame oil, and minced garlic to a food processor.
2. Process the ingredients until the dressing is fully incorporated and smooth. Continue blending briefly to whip air into the sauce, giving it a lighter body.
3. Transfer the finished dressing into a squeeze bottle for easy serving.
